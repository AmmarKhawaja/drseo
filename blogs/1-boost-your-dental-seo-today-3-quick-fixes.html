Boost Your Dental SEO Today: 3 Quick Fixes

JUNE 13, 2025 | AMMAR KHAWAJA, SEO SPECIALIST

Key Points

Optimize your Google Business Page by ensuring that all information is accurate, and include services you offer and original images.

Patient reviews are one of the most important parts of your online presence. Collect as many reviews as you can, and don’t forget to respond to new reviews.

Get backlinks from social media profiles and directories like Yelp and Zocdoc to boost credibility.

Online presence is more important for dentists than any other provider in the healthcare field, which is why Google SEO is crucial for dental practices that want to grow. SEO offers dentists a sustainable, long-term way to attract new patients and increase revenue. At Dental Rank SEO, we specialize in helping dentists improve their SEO, and we’ve compiled 5 actionable tips that dentists can implement today to boost their search ranking and get in front of more patients.

  1. Optimize Your Google Business Page

    One of the most important SEO steps happens off your website on your Google Business Page. This is often the first thing patients see when they search for a dentist, yet it’s frequently neglected by smaller practices.

    Start by making sure all your information is accurate and complete:

    • Website
    • Phone number
    • Business hours
    • Location
    • Services

    Google uses this data to rank you, and patients use it to contact you. In the Services section, be thorough and detailed. Images also matter. Use real photos of the dentist, your team, and your office. Avoid stock or AI-generated images, as they erode trust and can hurt your ranking.

  2. Get Patient Reviews

    Reviews live on your Google Business Page, but they’re so important they deserve their own focus. Even if everything else is perfect, few or negative reviews will hurt you. Patients want to see strong ratings and a healthy number of reviews.

    Encourage patients to leave a review:

    • Verbally
    • Through a follow‑up text or email
    • With small incentives like a discount or treat

    Once you start receiving reviews, respond to them. Thank patients for positive feedback and address negative ones promptly and with a solution if possible. Showing that you care about patient feedback builds trust and reputation.

  3. Build Local Backlinks

    Backlinks are typically tough to earn, but there are easy wins for dental practices. Start with getting your dental practice listed on these sites:

    • Google Business Page
    • Social Media Profiles
    • Yelp
    • Better Business Bureau
    • Zocdoc
    • Healthgrades
    • Vitals
